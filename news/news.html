NIST Cybersecurity

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) provides authoritative cybersecurity frameworks, guidelines, and best practices. It is a key resource for professionals looking to enhance security posture through standards like the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and NIST 800-series publications.

The Hacker News

A widely followed cybersecurity news website covering the latest threats, vulnerabilities, data breaches, and research developments. It is popular among security professionals for real-time updates on cyber incidents and emerging attack techniques.

Cyware Security News

A cybersecurity news aggregation platform that curates updates from multiple sources, providing insights into threats, intelligence, and industry trends. It is useful for staying informed about security developments in one centralized location.

CSIS Cyber Incidents

A resource maintained by the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS) that tracks major cyberattacks and breaches affecting governments, businesses, and critical infrastructure. It provides a historical record of high-impact incidents, helping analysts and policymakers understand long-term trends in cybersecurity.
